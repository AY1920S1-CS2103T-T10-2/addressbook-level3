DukeCooks

This is a lifestyle application. It’s designed for health-conscious individuals with the preference of using Command Line Interface (CLI). You’ll be able to manage various aspects of your life, such as your diet, health and exercise regime.

DukeCooks in your hands, get your life all planned!
